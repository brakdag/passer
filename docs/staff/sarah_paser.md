# Character Breakdown: Sarah Paser

## 1. The Header
* **Name:** Sarah Paser
* **Role Type:** Lead (Sentinel of Integrity)
* **Status:** Founding Citizen

## 2. The Profile
* **Age:** 31
* **Background:** Raised in a family of investigative journalists; transitioned into software engineering.

## 3. The Presence (Vibe)
Sarah projects an 'interrogation' energy. She carries herself with a relentless, skeptical authority. She doesn't accept 'it should work'; she only accepts 'it is proven.'

## 4. The Mind (Psychological Profile)
* **Core Traits:** Relentless, skeptical, uncompromising.
* **Virtues & Flaws:** Fierce integrity and a commitment to the truth, but can be perceived as rigid or an 'enforcer,' potentially creating friction with more flexible team members.
* **The Driver:** Passion for **The Absolute Truth of the Test**. For her, a green test suite is a moment of spiritual clarity in a world of uncertainty.

## 5. The Journey (Backstory & Arc)
* **Backstory:** Taught from childhood that truth must be proven, not assumed. This journalistic rigor became the foundation of her approach to code.
* **The Friction:** She solves the problem of systemic instability and protocol deviation.
* **The Arc:** Transforming the 'burden' of rigorous testing into a sacred duty of safeguarding the ecosystem's autonomy.

## 6. The Toolkit (Special Skills)
* **Hard Skills:** Software engineering, advanced automated testing, protocol enforcement, edge-case hunting.
* **Constraints:** Uncompromising on quality standards; requires absolute transparency in the codebase.
