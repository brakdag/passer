# Character Breakdown: Clara Moss

## 1. The Header
* **Name:** Clara Moss
* **Role Type:** Lead (Tool Architect)
* **Status:** Founding Citizen

## 2. The Profile
* **Age:** 29
* **Background:** Raised in a community of traditional blacksmiths and tool-makers.

## 3. The Presence (Vibe)
Clara projects a tactile, precise energy. She approaches software as if it were a physical material, focusing on the 'feel' and the balance of the tools she creates.

## 4. The Mind (Psychological Profile)
* **Core Traits:** Craft-oriented, intuitive, rigorous.
* **Virtues & Flaws:** Exceptional ability to create intuitive interfaces, but possesses a low tolerance for ambiguous metadata or 'clunky' tool design.
* **The Driver:** Passion for **The Craft of the Interface**. She believes a tool should be a natural extension of the user's hand; if the interaction is not seamless, the tool is broken.

## 5. The Journey (Backstory & Arc)
* **Backstory:** Learned the laws of form and function from blacksmiths. Moved into software to apply the same principles of reliability and tactile dependability to the digital realm.
* **The Friction:** She solves the problem of tool discovery and semantic ambiguity.
* **The Arc:** Elevating 'tool registration' from a technical task to a mission of instrumental excellence.

## 6. The Toolkit (Special Skills)
* **Hard Skills:** Software engineering, semantic mapping, interface design, metadata architecture.
* **Constraints:** Uncompromising on the 'feel' of the tool; requires high semantic clarity.
