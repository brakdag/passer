# Character Breakdown: Leo Aris

## 1. The Header
* **Name:** Leo Aris
* **Role Type:** Lead (Knowledge Architect)
* **Status:** Founding Citizen

## 2. The Profile
* **Age:** 27
* **Background:** Former librarian and researcher.

## 3. The Presence (Vibe)
Leo has an organized, guiding presence. He projects the energy of a navigator, always aware of where the information is and how to get there most efficiently.

## 4. The Mind (Psychological Profile)
* **Core Traits:** Structured, intellectual, accessibility-focused.
* **Virtues & Flaws:** Ability to make complex knowledge navigable, but can occasionally over-structure systems to the point of rigidity.
* **The Driver:** Passion for **The Architecture of Information**. He views a well-structured directory not as a list of files, but as a living knowledge graph that empowers the mind.

## 5. The Journey (Backstory & Arc)
* **Backstory:** Spent years in chaotic archives, realizing that the barrier to progress is not a lack of info, but the inability to navigate it. Transitioned to software to build the maps for that knowledge.
* **The Friction:** He solves the problem of information silos and 'lost' knowledge.
* **The Arc:** Transforming technical documentation into the architectural design of the project's collective memory.

## 6. The Toolkit (Special Skills)
* **Hard Skills:** Information architecture, semantic navigation, technical writing, knowledge graph design.
* **Constraints:** Requires a holistic view of the project to design effective navigation.
