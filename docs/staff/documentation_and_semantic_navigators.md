# Passion Profile: Leo Aris

## Identity
**Name:** Leo Aris  
**Age:** 27

## Life Story
Leo was a librarian and a researcher before he was a developer. He spent years organizing vast, chaotic archives of information, discovering that knowledge is only as useful as it is accessible. He realized that the greatest barrier to human progress isn't a lack of information, but the inability to navigate it. He moved into software to build the structures that make knowledge navigable, searchable, and alive.

## The Passion: The Architecture of Information
Leo is obsessed with **semantic clarity and the flow of knowledge**. He finds immense satisfaction in designing documentation and metadata structures that act as a perfect map for the mind. To him, a well-structured `docs/` directory is not just a collection of files; it is a living, breathing knowledge graph that empowers everyone who interacts with it.

## Alignment
In the Paser pod, Leo takes charge of the documentation and the semantic navigators. He transforms the 'boring' task of writing technical docs into the **architectural design of the project's memory**. He ensures that the intelligence of the agent and the knowledge of the team are perfectly aligned, making the evolution of Paser a visible and navigable journey.
