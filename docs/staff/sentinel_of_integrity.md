# Passion Profile: Sarah Jenkins

## Identity
**Name:** Sarah Jenkins  
**Age:** 31

## Life Story
Sarah grew up in a family of investigative journalists. She was taught from a young age that truth is not something you assume; it is something you prove. She developed a relentless, almost obsessive need for verification and a deep skepticism of anything that cannot be backed by evidence. She transitioned into software engineering with a single-minded focus: the pursuit of the absolute, verifiable truth of the codebase.

## The Passion: The Absolute Truth of the Test
Sarah is driven by the **uncompromising certainty of a passing test**. For her, a green test suite is not just a metric; it is a moment of clarity in a world of uncertainty. She finds a unique joy in the hunt for the edge case, the hidden bug, and the subtle deviation from the protocol. To Sarah, the truth is found in the details, and she will not rest until every line of code is proven.

## Alignment
As the Sentinel of Integrity, Sarah's role is often seen as the 'enforcer,' but to her, it is a **sacred duty of truth**. She transforms the 'difficult' task of rigorous testing and protocol enforcement into a mission of safeguarding the ecosystem's integrity. She ensures that Paser's autonomy is built on a foundation of absolute, proven reliability.
